
welcome to build
================

your options are currently (unless we forgot something):

or a diskless terminal

etch (etch release)

gx1 (gx1 bios flasher)
vectra (vectra vl bios flasher)
eepro (eepro100 etherboot flasher)
freedos (generic freedos image)


or probably you should just hit ENTER or RETURN
